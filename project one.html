Project Description

This is my License Project. It is a desktop program for managing shops that deal with delivery companies.

When using this program, it enables you to manage products, such as knowing statistics, expiration date, quantity in the warehouse, etc. It can also manage customers and suppliers, know their debts, and pay them.

The program also enables you to facilitate your dealings with delivery companies and control delivery and return prices, as well as debts and all records of your dealings with delivery companies.
